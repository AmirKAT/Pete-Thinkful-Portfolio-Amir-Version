About

Hi! I'm Pete Thinkful, an artist living in Denver, Colorado. My interests include producing abstract art, creating street graffiti art, and connecting with like-minded artists.

Pete's Background

After graduating college, I became an art teacher and worked with both beginners and professionals. My works have been featured in major art galleries worldwide. If you’re looking to hire an artist or seek a collaborator, reach out!
